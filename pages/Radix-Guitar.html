About

Guitars Indonesia founded on a solid ground of long experience in designs and production of the best Indonesian guitars. Endless spirit to produce better guitars every moment is always at the heart of every Radix team. Respect to the beauty and the tradition of a beautiful musical instrument called guitar and passion to continually create “The Best Indonesian Guitar” is the biggest thing in every Radix team’s mind. We believe that we have to push ourselves far beyond our limitations to proudly say that these guitars are “made in Indonesia”. We will never stop at the point probably called as ‘perfection’...


Piles of designs and prototypes, hundreds production methods, thousands professional gigs, and countless hours of discussions taught us how to build this beautiful instrument. Radix in your hands could tell you a lot about all of these endless spirit, passion, determination, love and care handed to it...
